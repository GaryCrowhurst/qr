üì¶ Batch Generation Tutorial

Create Multiple QR Codes at Once
  1. Click "Batch CSV" tab
  2. Create a CSV file with format: name,url
  3. Example: Student1,https://portfolio1.com
  4. Upload your CSV file
  5. Click "Generate Batch"
  6. Download ZIP with all QR codes!
